About us

Welcome to Le Garden Cafe, where the charm of autumn lives all year round. Since 1999, we’ve been a peaceful retreat in the heart of the city—inviting you to slow down, breathe, and enjoy life’s simple moments.

Our story

In the fall of 1999, our founder—an old soul with a deep love for coffee, poetry, and golden gardens—stood beneath a maple tree and imagined a space where people could pause, connect, and find warmth in both drink and spirit. Inspired by the calm, reflective beauty of autumn, he envisioned a café that would feel like a timeless garden in mid-October—rich with color, comfort, and calm. He opened Le Garden Cafe with just a few wooden tables, fresh pastries made by hand, and coffee brewed with heart. Over time, word spread—not just about the quality of the food and drinks, but about the feeling. The quiet conversations. The rustle of leaves outside. The peace. Today, Le Garden Cafe blends cozy garden vibes with a touch of elegance. Surrounded by warm colors, natural textures, and soft music, our space is perfect for relaxed mornings, romantic evenings, or quiet afternoons of reading and reflection. We invite you to be part of our story—where every cup tells a story, every visit feels like a gentle pause, and every guest is home.
